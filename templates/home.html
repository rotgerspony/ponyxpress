PonyXpress

Lightweight field routing + scanning. Use the Map to drop stops, the Scanner to capture parcels, and Admin to manage data.

Quick Start

  1. Login: alice or admin (password: password).
  2. On Map, click “Locate Me”, drop pins, Save.
  3. On Scanner, scan and Save; optional auto-create mailbox stops.

Roles

carrier: save stops & scans · substitute: view only · admin: full control.
