About

PonyXpress is a tiny Flask app for field routes: drop map stops, scan parcels, and manage data fast.

  • Map (Mapbox GL) with deep-linking, admin tools, and optional routing preview.
  • Scanner (Quagga2) with auto-save and camera picker.
  • SQLite persistence with DB export/backup/restore.
